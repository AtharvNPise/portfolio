About Me

I'm Atharv Pise, a passionate Technical Support Engineer currently working at Netweb Technology India Ltd., where I manage live data center infrastructure and help clients deploy enterprise-grade computing solutions.

My journey in electronics and telecommunications, combined with specialized training in High Performance Computing and System Administration (HPCSA) from C-DAC, has given me a unique blend of hardware-level expertise and software automation skills.
